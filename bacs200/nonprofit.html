Their Strategies

  • Khan Academy believes in a free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. Instead of ads or subscriptions, they are supported by individual contributions from generous people across the world with the same vision as them.
  • They focus on skill mastery to help learners establish strong foundations, so there's no limit to what people can learn next!
  • They are working hard to ensure that Khan Academy empowers coaches of all kinds to better understand what their children or students are up to and how best to help them.
